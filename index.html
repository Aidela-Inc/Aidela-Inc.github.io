Why Choose Us?

Every solution we provide is rooted in the belief that we are at our best when we are working together to help others achieve their goals.

Software Engineering

Custom cloud analytic, web and mobile application development leveraging open source, industry best practices, covering all major languages and frameworks. AWS Web Service integration, DevSecOps, and agile methods to mitigate risk and meet all mission critical requirements.

System Engineering

Capture & analyze requirements, design new concepts, develop product strategy, streamline and securely automate manual business process, prototype, integrate, and document SOPs ensuring solutions are manageable and repeatable.

Data Science

Transform overwhelming amounts of data into actionable intelligence. Utilize AI and Machine Learning to mine, model, analyze and uncover complex relationships that impact mission objectives.

Open Source

Leverage open source technology to encourage collaboration and innovate faster than ever before. Support the kind of creativity and interoperability needed to maximize options while respecting budget.

Careers

We strongly support the achievement of personal career goals and a healthy work-life balance by encouraging conference attendance, self study, open source contributions, or prototype development.

Like you, we need our downtime with family and friends, personal hobbies and endeavors. Time away from work allows for personal enrichment and renewal. We encourage time away from work for personal enrichment and recharge. We know that a healthy mind is a sharp mind.

Lastly, giving back to our communities is a core part of who we are. We volunteer both our time and funds to local charities. At AIDELA, we know that each person can truly make a difference in the lives of others.

Our Leadership Team

AIDELA’s founders have over 30 years of experience innovating, integrating, and simplifying products and services from concepts to operations. Our commercial and federal industry partners know they can depend on us to get the job done – regardless of how large or complex the task.

Christine Mallick

Chief Executive Officer

Christine is the founder, owner, and CEO of AIDELA, Inc., a principal engineer, system architect, and solution strategist. She has over 20 years of experience in Cloud Architecture, Data Transformation and Curation, Data Management, Program and Project Management, and Information Security.

Ms. Mallick takes pride in cultivating diverse, multidisciplinary teams focused on innovation, efficiency and simplicity. Ms. Mallick graduated from the University of Maryland, Baltimore County with a Bachelors in Information Systems Management. She received her Masters in Information and Telecommunication Systems concentrating in Information Security from Johns Hopkins University.

Rich Giomundo

Chief Technology Officer

Richard Giomundo is co-founder and partner of AIDELA, Inc. Mr. Giomundo has broad technical knowledge and over 20 years of experience in Software Architecture Design, Enterprise Application Development and Technical Leadership.

Mr. Giomundo graduated with a Bachelors in Computer Science and Mathematics from the University at Buffalo; he went on to receive a Masters in Computer Science and Computer Security.
